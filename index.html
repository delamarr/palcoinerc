Free Palestine ($PAL)

Bridging Hope and Blockchain

Palestine Coin (PAL) is not just a cryptocurrency; it's a revolutionary movement, a digital manifestation of solidarity, and a beacon of hope. In a world where technology converges with social consciousness, Palestine Coin emerges as a symbol of unity, support, and resilience. This white paper outlines the vision, purpose, and potential impact of Palestine Coin, along with innovative marketing strategies to foster widespread adoption.

Vision and purpose

The vision of Palestine Coin goes beyond the financial realm. It aspires to create a global community that stands in solidarity with the people of Palestine. The purpose is twofold: to provide tangible aid through blockchain-based donations and to spark conversations around the Palestinian cause. Regular donations from the tax will be made to various Childrens’ charities around the globe like UNICEF.
